Digestive Health

Digestive health is important for
the well being of overall health

Digestion is a process by which our system breaks the food we eat to convert it as essential nutrients. These nutrients are energy for the micro unit, cells of each organ and the whole body.

All the food and drink is digested with the help of digestive enzymes.The first being our saliva when we chew the food and make into smaller portions that goes through the process of digestion from stomach to intestines mixing up with essential enzymes to get converted as parts of nutrients before it mixes with the blood and gets supplied to every cell of the body. The waste is excreted out as human waste.

This reiterates, you are what you eat and what's absorbed by your body. It's your choice of food, balanced nutrition, adequate water intake, status of digestive health and gut health for absorption. All really matters!!

Indigestion is expressed as many signs for different reasons and can be one or multiple signs felt during poor digestive health. Enzyme disorder, stomach medium disorder, gas, bloating, belching, spasms, irritation, leak of the gut, constipation and some chronic diseases too.

Choice of food, portion, time, method of eating, your immunity, mind status and importantly adequate sleep matters to keep your digestive health at best!

Some of the known toxins like alcohol, smoke and self medication should be avoided to keep the digestive system healthy!

    Probiotics are live bacteria or yeasts that are good, especially the digestive system.Probiotics are known as"good" or "helpful" bacteria which keep the gut healthy.

    Probiotics are live microorganisms which promotes a healthy digestive tract and a healthy immune system.

    Probiotics are supplements that contain the healthy bacteria similar to what's already there in digestive tract. These good bacteria help digestion and absorption of food and prevent gas formation which keeps off bloating during digestion. Occasionally, excess probiotics itself may cause certain discomforts like bloating.

    Lactobacillus acidophilus is the most common type of probiotic strain that helps boost the immune system. It also helps lactose-intolerance issues by digesting the dairy products better and helps with overall digestion.

    There are many types of probiotics strains. They include

    1. Lactobacilli family
      1. Lactobacillus acidophilus
      2. Lactobacillus Sporogenes
      3. Lactobacillus Rhamnosus
      4. Lactobacillus Reuterri
      5. Lactobacillus Plantarim
    2. Bifidobacteria family
      1. Bifidobacteria family
      2. Bifidobacterium Bifidum
      3. Bifidobacterium Longum
      4. Bifidobacterium Infantis
      5. Streptococcus thermophilus and Saccharomyces boulardii

    Different probiotics strains have different benefits. While one may help treat gut health the other might help a vaginal health and a few strains multiple benefits.Before you start taking a probiotic supplement, talk to your health care provider to make sure that you get the treatment most likely to help.
